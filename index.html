Our Purpose

International Society for Universal Dialogue (ISUD) is a philosophical society whose purpose is to promote discussions of human rights, world peace, and preservation of the environment. With our society of scholars and philosophers we have made significant efforts in these areas. Since our establishment in the 1980s, we gotten recognition for our work accross the world and plan to continue for many years to come. We hope for you to join us on this journey where you can inquire in the "Contact" section above.
